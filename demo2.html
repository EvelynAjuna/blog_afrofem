Chocolate


Chocolate cake is a cake flavored with melted chocolate, cocoa powder, or both. From it’s delicious taste, to its rich texture,the chocolate cake is considered to be one of the best desserts of all time.Chocolate cake is made with chocolate.It can also include other ingredients such as fudge, vanilla creme, and other sweeteners.

Red Velvet


Red velvet is a term used to describe a type of cake (or cupcake) with a deep red to burgundy color.Simply, it tastes like a mashup of chocolate and vanilla cake. But really, it's not exactly like either. Its flavor isn't deep and dark enough to be considered chocolate, but it's not light enough to be considered vanilla. After you've tasted it, you won't think chocolate or vanilla anymore, but red velvet.

Strawberry


Strawberry cake is a cake that uses strawberry as a primary ingredient. Strawberries may be used in the cake batter, atop cakes and in a strawberry cake's frosting. Some are served chilled or partially frozen, and they are sometimes served as a Valentine's Day dish.
